Our Story

Eighteen Years of Practical Counsel

Founded in 2008 by a small group of litigators and corporate advisors, Kingswell Legal grew from a two-partner chambers into a full-service firm serving individuals, businesses and the Indian diaspora. Our approach has never changed: understand the problem fully before proposing the solution.

Today our team spans corporate law, litigation, family law and NRI services — bound by the same standard of clarity and accountability the firm was built on.
